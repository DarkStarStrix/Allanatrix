NexaCompute

NexaCompute Infrastructure

Disposable compute. Durable lineage. NexaCompute orchestrates data prep, distillation, distributed training, LLM-as-judge evaluation, dashboards, and deployment across any GPU provider. The platform remains available for reference but is no longer actively maintained.

  • End-to-end manifests for distillation, training, evaluation, and serving.
  • Modular stacks covering nexa_data, nexa_distill, nexa_train, nexa_eval, nexa_ui, and nexa_infra.
  • Provider-agnostic orchestration patterns for Lambda, CoreWeave, RunPod, AWS, and bespoke clusters.
  • Historical telemetry and cost tracking for reproducible scientific ML runs.
